ABOUT US

Our story, mission & values

Our Story

Empire of Fitness was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate fitness professionals who believed that Delhi deserved a world-class training facility that combined elite equipment, expert coaching, and an unmatched community culture.

What began as a 5,000 sq ft facility with 200 members has grown into a 25,000 sq ft empire serving over 5,000 active members — all driven by a single conviction: that every person has a champion within them.

Our Mission

To empower every individual to unlock their fullest physical and mental potential through exceptional coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and a community that celebrates every victory.

Our Values

Excellence — We hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do.

Community — Our members aren't just clients — they're family.

Integrity — Honest guidance, transparent pricing, and genuine care for your wellbeing.

Innovation — We integrate the latest in sports science, nutrition, and recovery.

Our Facility

Located in the heart of Connaught Place, New Delhi, Empire of Fitness spans multiple floors featuring dedicated strength, cardio, combat, yoga, and recovery zones. Our nutrition bar offers certified sports nutrition and meal planning services.
